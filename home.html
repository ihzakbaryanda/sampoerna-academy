About Sampoerna Academy

Sampoerna Academy is a STEAM-focused school opening classes from pre-kindergarten to secondary levels. The school has six campuses located in Jakarta, BSD, Medan, Sentul and Surabaya.

As a school founded in Indonesia, Sampoerna Academy is a Cambridge certified school and follows the Cambridge curriculum. Furthermore, SA enriches content with STEAM and other internationally recognized curricula. On the other hand, the SA curriculum is also in line with the National Curriculum (2013).

STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Math) is a learning method designed to integrate science, technology, engineering, art, and math skills. This approach aims to teach students to think critically and creatively by mastering engineering and design principles through solving real-world problems while building their core competencies.
