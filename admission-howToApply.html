How To Apply?

To confirm your child’s enrollment, we request parents to provide the following application requirements:


  • Copies of school records for the past two years
  • A copy of Birth Certificate
  • Copies of ID (KTP & KK) or passports (cover page, picture page, expiration date and current visa page) of the students and parents
  • For international students, copies of KITAS (picture page and expiration date) of the students and parents
